shortbread cookies

Here is how to make shortbread cookies:
  1. leave 100 grams of butter in room temperature.
  2. mix with one whisked egg
  3. stir in 100 grams of sugar, then 130 grams of flour
  4. shape the dough like a cylinder, use a knife to slice the cookie dough, and bake!
